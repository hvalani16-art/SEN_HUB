what SEN means

SPECIAL EDUCATIONAL NEEDS (SEN) is a term used to describe students who require extra support or different teaching methods to help them learn and reach their full potential.

what autism and ADHD

specialized guidance for neurodivergent learners to help them manage focus, social communication, and sensory needs in a classroom environment.

Support Strategies

we provide tailored intervention plans designed to meet individual learning styles.our approach focuses on scaffolding tasks, using visual supports, and implementing positive reinforcement to help every student build confidence and achieve their goals.

SENSORY-FRIENDLY LEARNING

We create a low-stimulus environment designed to minimize sensory overload. By utilizing adjustable lighting, noise-reduction tools, and flexible seating, we ensure that every student can focus comfortably and engage with the curriculum at their own pace.
